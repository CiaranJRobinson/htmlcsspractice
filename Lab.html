Shiba Inus

The dog with the sass

Shiba facts:

  • National monument in Japan
  • Come in a few different coat colors
  • Usually stay between 20-25 lbs
  • Extremely loyal
  • Independent, don't need a lot of attention and unlikely to have seperation anxiety
  • Heavy shedders, be prepared with a strong vaccuum!
  • High prey drive, not good around small animals or off leash.
  • Originally used as a hunting dog
  • Incredibly smart, need lots of stimulation, puzzles, and regular daily obedience training (otherwise they'll ignore commands entirely!)
  • Energy levels moderate, good for apartments or small living spaces with daily walks
  • Escape artists!! Secure doorways and fences, they can climb!
  • Very healthy, don't have many hereditary problems
  • Ancient breed, evidence found as far back as 300BC of families owning them
